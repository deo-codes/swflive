About SWF Wrestling

Superstars of Wrestling Federation (SWF) Live Pro Wrestling is owned and was founded by 25-year pro wrestling veteran “Unbreakable” Rob Fury. Fury's vision for the brand is a pro wrestling environment where all generations collide. SWF Live is based on the East Coast (NJ/NY) and has entertained thousands of people throughout the years. Our fan experience is like no other. We host professional wrestling events, fundraisers, birthday parties, and much more. Come see for yourself what SWF Live Pro Wrestling has to offer!
